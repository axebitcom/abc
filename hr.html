Careers @ axebit

It’s an audacious, incredibly rewarding mission that our increasingly diverse team is dedicated to achieving.

Axebit offers talented and motivated people the opportunity to work on latest programming technologies in a dynamic working environment. Through internal advancement opportunities, competitive salaries, benefits, and a chance to work with customers around the world, you’ll have the opportunity to create a career that you can be proud of.

Exciting challenges lie ahead—new arenas, technologies, and businesses. Guided by our four core values, we’ll meet these challenges creatively and with the support of our global community. Join us!

Software Engineers (freshers)

We have a couple of vacancies for fresh Software Engineers.

Must have a Bachelors in Computer Science or Software Engineering.In the interview we will ask you to write code on paper, so you must be fully prepared and comfortable doing that. We specialize in web development using the cutting edge technologies like Mean stack, NodeJs, Ruby on Rails, Vue.JS, ReactJS and AngularJS. You must be good communicator in English and should be willing to do late night meetings with clients.

Ruby on Rails Developers (experienced)

We are looking for an experienced Ruby on rails developer for its Lahore office. We are seeking an individual who is passionate and dedicated towards providing quality work and who does not compromise quality at any cost. The individual will be expected to work with a team of experienced resources and will get a chance to flourish and contribute towards company goals. Please make sure you meet the understated requirements.

  • Detailed Understanding of web product development process
  • Minimum 2 years’ experience developing ruby on rails applications using best practices.
  • Understanding of the Model View Controller (MVC) design pattern and concepts of application to Ruby on Rails
  • Experienced in Mongo db.
  • Familiar with both Linux/Unix and windows environment.
  • Expert in Object Oriented analysis, design and development.
  • Experience building web front ends with HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and frameworks such as JQuery.
  • Experience with designing / building and consuming REST API’s.

Business Development Executive (experienced)
