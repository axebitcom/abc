History of axebit:

The story of Axebit

My story or the story of axebit is very simple. The inception of creating a developing company started when I was working as a software engineer in another development company which was actually a renowned company for in the dev circles, when we used to develop software for clients we were only concerned with the requirements of that software nothing else. If a client would ask us to do a dumb thing for his app, we would do that, and nothing else (period) we were never concerned with the with the success of the client. This thing bothered me a lot. For some reasons I moved to another dev house but the story was the same there. Many a time I would see a client asking some feature would would actually slow down its app to a disastrous extend, but we would develop it. The client was paying us to develop.

I created this company with a motto that this dev house would lead to the success of app, we would develop the applications for clients which would make them successful. My mantra for this company is My client success is my success

- M Abdullah
CEO, Axebit Co
